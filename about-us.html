Scholars in Sound - FMSA

Our Purpose

Farmington Music Student Association is a student-founded organization with a mission to share our music with a wider audience. We are dedicated to showcasing our impact on the lives of those involved in our association, providing a platform for students to express their talent and gain valuable performance experience. Join us in our musical journey!

Our Journey

Founded with a passion for music, our association has grown to become a vibrant community of music enthusiasts. From humble beginnings to impactful performances, our history is a testament to our commitment to musical excellence.

Our Influence

Through our music, we aim to inspire, uplift, and connect with our audience. The impact of our performances goes beyond the notes played - it resonates with the hearts and minds of those who experience our music.
